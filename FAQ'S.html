2Do I Need Any Prior Yoga Experience?

We chose PRIME Fitness USA for the Warhouse Gym because we want to give our members, our guests, our trainers, and of course ourselves the opportunity to be able to take athletic performance to the next level. The adjustable SmartCam and 3-peg Torque Arm design help you to train “smarter” by incorporating the use of the strength curve. Very few exercises, if any, maintain high tension on a muscle throughout its entire range of motion. Because of joint angles, the resistance or tension of an exercise cannot be constant through the entire rep. Most exercises have strength curves where tension is high only in specific portions of the range of motion, but by using PRIME’s innovative SmartStrength technology, you are able to train your muscle and give tension through the full range of motion. It’s also great for working on sticking points of exercises and helps you to recruit more muscle fibers that you would not be recruiting on traditional equipment. Train smarter. Train to get stronger. And maximize your time in the gym! Thank you PRIME Fitness! We love you and our members love you!

3What If I Get Tired?

I have invested in PRIME equipment to get the best out of my coaching results. Many Olympians get referred to me to help them deal with injuries. The biggest advantage of the SmartStrength feature, is that is allows me to match the strength curve of the athlete for a full range of motion. Their Plate Loaded Leg Curl is my favorite in dealing with hamstrings tears, as I can get the athlete to do full range in record time, and rebuild the strength accordingly. Because of the incompetence in the strength and conditioning world, I see a lot of slap tears. There again, a resistance training machine that allows to overload specific patterns of the strength curve is critical. Not only is PRIME ultra-valuable in the rehab process, the company also builds very sturdy, efficient pieces to maximize strength training.

8Can I use the studio away from Personal Training appointments?

The studio is used for Personal Training appointments only. There is no access outside of these sessions.

9How much does a Personal Training session cost?

You can find out more about pricing via the enquiry form below.

10Is parking provided? And is it free?

Parking is provided and is free. There is an open, well kept gravel courtyard which can hold nearly 20 vehicles, providing ample parking for your visit.

11What is your cancellation policy?

For Personal Training sessions we have a 24 hour cancellation policy. If you cancel your session within 24 hours of the session starting, we can charge you the full session price for late notice. We do understand however that circumstances out of your control do occur, and we therefore review this on a case by case basis.

12Do you offer online training?

Yes we do have an online offering. We are able to provide Personal Training online via Zoom, FaceTime, WhatsApp video etc. reducing the limitations of physical location. Consultations can also be taken online over video.

13Is a changing area available?

There are no changing rooms provided. However, the toilets do provide space to change if necessary. There is no access to a shower.

14Are there toilets?

Yes there are. They are situated directly next to the studio and can be accessed at all times.
